Fun Facts about places I've been

World Globe !
  • I have never went on Vacation with a detailed itenerary, it feels to cookie cutter for me so I like to just see whats there the day I arrive and plan!
  • ALWAYS talk to the locals, they dont bite and normally will offer some piece of useful information!
  • I try to get a picture with a dog in every country I go too!
  • Coffee is good in EVERY country I have been to thus far!
  • Google is your best friend!

How to live in the moment

RELAX !
  • EAT the local food!
  • Be thankful and blessed for the oppurtunity to not be standing in your own backyard!
  • Truly stop and listen for more than a minute of all the noises around you, take deep breaths, and see as far as the eye can!
  • PUT THE PHONE DOWN, until you REALLY need it!

Travel on a Budget

Book Flights !
  • Buy your flights on Tuesday/Wednesday, thay are the cheapest!
  • Pack light, you can always wash clothes!
  • View everything as an oppurtunity!
  • Travel during off peak times, see the place for what it really is!
